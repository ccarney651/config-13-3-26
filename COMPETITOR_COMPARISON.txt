GARDEN ROOM CONFIGURATOR — COMPETITOR COMPARISON
=================================================
Competitor: gardenroomplanner.com/app
Updated: April 2026


OVERVIEW
--------
The competitor is a mature, feature-dense configurator covering the full
customer journey from exterior shell to kitchen drawer handles. Our tool
has a strong pricing engine, solid 3D foundation, and a cleaner UI — but
is missing entire product categories that customers would expect to
configure before generating a quote.

The gap is not in quality of what we have, but in breadth. Areas like the
kitchen tab, decking shapes, sauna, and dynamic 3D electrical placement
represent months of development work and will be the most visible shortfalls
to a customer coming from the competitor.

SUMMARY: PRIORITY GAP LIST
---------------------------
Ranked by customer impact:

1. KITCHEN TAB
   Biggest visible gap. Affects all studio/office/annexe use cases.
   Scope: new tab, resizable cabinet system, 5 sub-categories,
   finish/worktop/handle customisation.

2. 3D ELECTRICAL PLACEMENT
   Every socket, switch, and light as a placeable wall object.
   Significant work — scene.js wall-snapping system needed.
   Current qty counters still feed pricing correctly.

3. DECKING SHAPES AND MULTI-PIECE
   6 shape presets, unlimited placed pieces, per-object resize and
   balustrade options. Currently we have a single area slider.

4. EXTERIOR COLOUR PER ELEMENT
   Separate colour pickers for Windows, Posts, Flashing, Guttering,
   Door Handles. Currently one global frame colour picker.

5. SAUNA PRESET + OBJECTS
   Preset room with adjustable bench, sauna-specific fixtures.
   Depends on employer providing GLB models.

6. CLOAKROOM PRESET
   Simple — bathroom minus shower. GLB models needed.

7. BLINDS ON OPENINGS
   Per-opening blinds toggle in 4 colours. Primarily a 3D visual feature.

8. STRUCTURE TYPES (Partial Opening, Attached Extension)
   Major geometry work — alters the exterior shell model fundamentally.

9. ENVIRONMENT SCENES (ground texture presets)
   Low effort, cosmetic. Grass, paved, gravel, sand.

10. ADDITIONAL ROOF SLIDERS
    Thickness and trim height on flat/pitched roofs. Minor.


WHAT WE DO BETTER
-----------------
- Pricing engine accuracy and admin override system
- Per-wall cladding overrides (may not exist in competitor)
- Save/Load/Share design system
- Onboarding overlay for new customers
- Cleaner, faster UI with no framework overhead
- PDF quote export (print-to-PDF)
- Lead capture with full spec in email body
